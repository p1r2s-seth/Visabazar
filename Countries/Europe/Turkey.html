No eye glasses or caps. Women should tie their hair properly. If you want us to file an online application, send digital photographs only. Ask the photographer to email you.

→ Bank Statement - Last 6 months saving account bank statements signed and attested by a bank office. In case the applicant is self-employed then the Company's last 6 months bank statement is also required. All bank statements must be signed by the bank & dated within the last one week of application submission.

→ Income Tax Returns - ITRs along with a computation chart for the last 3 years.
Businessman - Personal ITRs as well as for your company.
Proprietor - Personal 3 Years ITRs.
Employed - Form16 for the last 3 years.

→ If the applicant has not enough funds or bank account, a financial support letter from the sponsor notarized. (Not applicable for spouses, parents and children)

FAQs

1What is a B2/B1 visa to US?

The B2/B1 US visa is a non-immigrant visa which allows foreign nationals to enter into the U.S. temporarily for activities like tourism, visiting friends and relatives, medical care, participation in conventions, conferences, or convocations of fraternal or social organizations, participation by amateurs in musical, sports and similar events.

2 How long in advance should I apply for a US Visa?

Even though most US Visa applications are processed in 2-3 business days, in case additional processing is required, it may take up to 12 weeks. Moreover it is difficult to get appointments as well. Therefore, you should plan to apply for your US Visa as soon as possible or around 3 months in advance.

3Is it possible to get a multiple-entry USA visa to the United States?

Yes! As per the recent amendments to U.S. Immigration Laws, it is possible to get a multi-entry USA visa. With this visa, you can come and go from the United States for countless times without need to reapply every time you visit the U.S. The validity of the multiple-entry USA visa may last up to 10 years, which means you only have to apply for another US visa once in a decade.

4 My passport containing a valid US Visa has expired and I've obtained a new passport. Do I need a new US visa now?

If the passport in which your US visa is endorsed has expired, the visa in the old passport can still be used, provided you carry a valid passport of the same nationality along with the expired passport.
